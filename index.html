Supported Card Types

  • Visa: Starts with 4, 13-19 digits, 3-digit CVV
  • MasterCard: Starts with 51-55 or 2221-2720, 16 digits, 3-digit CVV
  • American Express: Starts with 34 or 37, 15 digits, 4-digit CVV
  • Discover: Starts with 65 or 6011, 16-19 digits, 3-digit CVV

    • *Year/Month cant be in the past or more than 10 years in the future*
